Faq

Soru Cevap
Why Kids are missing from some festivals Some children were temporarily excluded from some festivals due to compatibility adjustments. This is mainly because I'm having a hard time finding a suitable place for the kids' map changes. The important thing now is to have kids at important events like Winter Star. It might be a little bit missing in the fall season.
Why is Tina's skin color so white? I'm not an illustrator and almost none of the designs in the Stardew kids Expansion mod (except for the maps) are mine. When Tina was first designed I asked an illustrator on Discord to help me and he made this drawing, I'm not even a good enough illustrator to change it. That's why Tina's skin color is so white in the main mod for now. You can use a separate portrait mode to change this. if you make a new tina drawing and send it to me I will be happy to publish it.
Where are the kid's rooms? Every kids's have family has a room in their home and to enter these rooms you have to use doors that are mostly built into the walls. If you don't see the doors, it might be because you are using a different mod that changes the interior maps. If you have a mod you would like me to make compatible please leave me a comment with the mod name and a description. I can't try all the mods that change the internal maps one by one to see which mods the mod is compatible with.For now it is compatible with SVE.
If I marry a wizard/witch, will Douglas move into my house? No, no, no. There's no way I can do that. Douglas will continue to live in the wizard's tower. Maybe in the future I can try different things to change this, but I will try that after the 1.6 update.And no, I still can't send Douglas directly to your farmhouse, that's one of the things that the game code doesn't allow.
Is this mod not being updated? The reason I don't update as often is that I'm currently studying at university and I don't have as much time for this as I used to because of the responsibilities of normal life. Nevertheless, I continue mod designing as a hobby, so this mod continues to receive updates, just slower.
